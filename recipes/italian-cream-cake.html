Italian cream cake

Description

This is a cake that I have not made!

I got the inspiration to look up this cake recipe from a memory of my partner buying a sponge cake with cream from an Italian cafe. I think what was good about the cake that we ate was the texture of the cake as a whole when you bit through the sponge and moist cream

Ingredients

  • 1 C buttermilk
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ C butter
  • ½ C shortening
  • 2 C white sugar
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 C flaked coconut
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 C all-purpose flour
  • 8 oz cream cheese
